I'm a software engineer specialized in forging high-end digital solutions.
I'm originally from Douala, Cameroon. I owe much of my career to the Web
and the global Open Source community.

developer experience (DX).

My work now centers on bridging the gap between complex backend logic and
seamless, fluid user interfaces that feel as robust under the hood as they
look on the surface.

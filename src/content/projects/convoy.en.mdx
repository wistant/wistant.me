# Convoy: Enterprise Webhook Delivery

**Convoy** is the complete solution for secure, scalable, and reliable webhook delivery. Designed for developers and trusted by enterprises, it simplifies the complex architecture of managing incoming and outgoing events. Our platform ensures that event-driven communication never fails.

![Convoy Platform Landing](/portfolio/convoy/convoy.landing.png)

## Advanced Endpoint Management

Enhance your webhook reliability, security, and performance. Our advanced endpoint controls are meticulously tailored to ensure stable, secure, and efficient message delivery preventing well-known attacks like SSRF (Server-Side Request Forgery). 

![Convoy Features Architecture](/portfolio/convoy/concoy.features.png)

Whether you are routing millions of payloads per second or securing private network endpoints, the infrastructure handles the complexity for you.

## Unparalleled Developer Experience

We believe developer tooling should be as intuitive as it is powerful. 

- **Rolling Secrets & Signatures**: Create secrets, sign payloads, and mathematically verify events to maintain the absolute highest level of cryptographic security.
- **Dynamic Filtering**: Route events intelligently without writing custom middleware. Save engineering hours.
- **Playground**: The Convoy Playground makes testing webhook integrations effortless. Generate webhook URLs, inspect payloads, and analyze headers—all in one place.

![Core Product View](/portfolio/convoy/convoy.product.png)

## Highly Reliable Architecture

Built for resilience and absolute speed, the system combines a robust control and data plane architecture powered by PostgreSQL. This guarantees high availability, durability, and seamless data handling at enterprise scale. 

Our compliance posture is unbreakable:
- SOC2 Compliant
- GDPR Compliant
- Custom SSO & SAML
- Private Networking Environments
- 99.999% uptime SLA with Role-based access control.

# Shopdo: Modern E-Commerce Platform 🛒

Shopdo bridges the gap between content marketing and retail. It's a complete e-commerce solution engineered with a dynamic Next.js storefront, a scalable shopping cart system, and most importantly, an integrated blog platform.

## Storefront Architecture

![Shopdo Homepage Frontend](/portfolio/shopdo-store.vercel.app.webp)

The storefront is fully SSR (Server-Side Rendered) to ensure instant load times and maximum SEO scoring across all major search engines.

## Integrated Content Marketing

![Shopdo Blog Grid](/portfolio/shopdo-blog2.webp)

Instead of relying on a separate WordPress instance, Shopdo seamlessly integrates technical content alongside product pages.

This allows the brand to seamlessly weave product links into their articles without users ever leaving the SPA (Single Page Application) context, significantly improving conversion rates.

# ShopDO: The Electronics E-Commerce Hub 🛒

Grab Up to 50% off on selected headphones! ShopDO is a highly scalable, robust e-commerce frontend built specifically for handling vast amounts of consumer electronics data—from Apple AirPods to Canon DSLRs.

## Popular Categories & Smart Filtering

![ShopDO Home Catalog](/portfolio/shopdo/shopdo-store.vercel.app.webp)

A successful digital storefront demands incredibly fast filtering and robust availability tracking. 
ShopDO seamlessly organizes thousands of products into functional categories:
- Kitchen Appliances
- Television & Refrigerators
- Washing Machines
- Gadgets & Smartphones (like the iPhone 16 Pro Max 128GB or Samsung Galaxy S25 Ultra 5G)

Each category tracks real-time stock levels (e.g., "(11) Products of this category is Available"), displaying localized FCFA currency logic flawlessly.

## The Blog Ecosystem Integration

Beyond pure shopping, ShopDO incorporates a rich editorial experience to drive organic SEO and customer engagement.

![ShopDO Latest Blog Items](/portfolio/shopdo-blog/shopdo-blog2.webp)

The integrated blog system supports various categories such as Lifestyle, Social Media, Company News, and Electronics, keeping the user engaged while significantly boosting search engine visibility. This creates a unified experience where content marketing and actual point-of-sale converge beautifully.

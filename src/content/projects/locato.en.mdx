# Locato: Find Faster, Rent Better

**Locato** is the definitive real estate platform for Cameroon, starting with the vibrant city of Douala. We simplify the entire rental process, from discovery to moving in.

![Locato Mobile Experience](/portfolio/locato/locato-.vercel.app.webp)

## The Story
Finding a quality studio or apartment in a busy city can be a nightmare. Locato was born to eliminate middlemen and bring transparency to the market. Whether you are a tenant looking for a home in Akwa or a landlord in Bonapriso, we bridge the gap.

## Seamless Workflow
- **Search**: Advanced filtering by neighborhood, price, and bed count.
- **Visit**: Schedule visits directly through our mobile application.
- **Apply**: Submit your documents securely via the platform.
- **Move In**: Sign your lease and move into your new home.

## Mobile First
Our app features a precision geolocation system, direct communication with owners, and 24/7 assistance to ensure a safe and verified experience.

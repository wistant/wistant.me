# Where Design Meets Motion

Ochi is a state-of-the-art digital showroom for a premium design agency. It leverages advanced motion engineering with <TechBadge name="React" /> and <TechBadge name="GSAP" /> to create a sensory-rich browsing experience.

## Orchestrated Interaction

Every interaction in Ochi is choreographed to reflect the agency's commitment to precision and avant-garde aesthetics. We used **Locomotive Scroll** and **Framer Motion** to ensure smooth scrolling physics and deep parallax effects.

## Performance & Optimization

Despite the heavy animations, Ochi maintains high performance through aggressive asset optimization and lazy-loading of WebGL components.

- **Vite-powered Build**: Lightning-fast HMR and optimized production bundles.
- **GSAP Timelines**: Centralized animation control for perfect synchronization.
- **Responsive Fluidity**: Seamlessly scaling from desktop widescreen to mobile touch interfaces.

# Machines With Future Technology 🏎️

Elysian Drive is a meticulously crafted landing page for a high-end electric car rental service. It caters to a luxurious audience by providing them with an immersive browsing experience for top-tier electric vehicles, largely focusing on the Porsche brand.

## The Electric Future

See the future with high-performance electric cars produced by renowned brands. They feature futuristic builds and designs with new and innovative platforms that last a long time. 

![Elysian Drive UI](/portfolio/elsyandrive/elsyandrive-wistant.netlify.app.webp)

Users can instantly view vital statistics for every car model in the fleet:
- **Temperature & Mileage**: (e.g., *873 MILEAGE*)
- **Battery**: Real-time mockups of remaining charges (*94% BATTERY*)
- **Acceleration & Top Speed**: (e.g., Porsche Taycan *2.8 Sec, 260 Km/h*)

## Exclusive Features & Superchargers

With an integrated design that boasts over 2,500 superchargers placed along popular routes, the platform reassures drivers about their journey's viability. Distinct features such as **800V Turbo Charging**, **350 Km Range**, and **480 Km Travel** capabilities are beautifully presented in dedicated UI segments to build trust and excitement.

Elysian Drive is designed to give the illusion of speed and exclusivity even before the client turns the key.

# Select: Supercharging Snowflake

**Select** is a highly specialized analytics and cost-management platform built natively for deep Snowflake integration. It supercharges thousands of data engineers by delivering absolute clarity over massive cloud data workflows and infrastructure expenditure.

![Select Landing Experience](/portfolio/select/select.landing.png)

## Unprecedented Instant Insights

Gain deep visibility into your entire Snowflake ecosystem. The platform isolates slow queries, audits unused tables, flags misconfigured warehouse clusters, and automates monumental infrastructure savings with the click of a button. 

![Select Platform Overview](/portfolio/select/select.png)

## Elite Cost Drivers Analysis

Integration is entirely frictionless. Within 30 minutes, without deploying heavy external agents, Select maps a full, granular view of your primary cost drivers. This features an unprecedented depth of analysis on `dbt` project expenditures—data that teams have historically struggled to extract using legacy BI approaches.

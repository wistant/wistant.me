# Nova: Enter the Metagame 🎮

**Nova** is a high-interactivity showcase for Zentry, the world's largest metagame layer. It’s not just a landing page; it’s an invitation to a new reality where play and life converge.

![Nova Realm Entrance](/portfolio/nova/nova.webp)

## The Story
Our mission with Zentry is to create a seamless metagame environment. Nova captures this complexity through a storytelling experience that evolves as the user scrolls, moving from the physical world into the digital realm.

## The Arsenal
- **Radiant**: A cross-platform metagame app that turns your gaming activities into a rewarding adventure.
- **Zigma**: An anime-inspired NFT collection that serves as the IP cornerstone for the entire ecosystem.

![Nova Arsenal](/portfolio/nova/nova3.webp)

## Nexus & Azul
- **Nexus**: A gamified social hub that brings Web3 communities together in a playable space.
- **Azul**: Your cross-world AI Agent, designed to make your gameplay more productive and incredibly fun.

![Nexus Social Hub](/portfolio/nova/nova4.webp)

## Technical Magic
Nova redefines digital behavior with **WebGL** masking and **GSAP** physics. Every scroll move triggers a response, creating a dynamic environment that feels alive and responsive.

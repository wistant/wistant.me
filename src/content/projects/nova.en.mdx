# Nova: Premium Agency Portfolio 🌌

Nova is not just a template; it is a statement. Designed for creative agencies pushing the boundaries of the digital space, Nova delivers a striking brutalist-inspired aesthetic combined with ultra-smooth scroll animations driven by GSAP and Next.js.

# Elementis: The Future of Luxury Wellness

At **Elementis**, we are not just building resorts; we are crafting a life-changing ecosystem where nature, innovation, and well-being converge. This project represents a bold vision for socially and emotionally conscious living.

![Elementis Sanctuary](/portfolio/elementis/1.elementis.png)

## The Story
Founded by Anton Titov and Andrey Skripachev, Elementis was born from a desire to foster meaningful connections and provide genuine care. Our mission is to inspire a global community that prioritizes sustainable construction and climate-responsive solutions without compromising on luxury.

## Innovative Solutions
We’ve integrated groundbreaking technologies to ensure an energy-efficient and healthy environment:
- **Sun-Ice Energy**: Utilizing world-first solar-powered thermal storage technology for cooling.
- **Glulam Construction**: Buildings crafted from responsibly sourced glue-laminated timber—eco-friendly, durable, and resistant to tropical moisture.
- **Low-E Solar Control Glass**: Specialized insulated glass that blends energy efficiency with breathtaking aesthetic appeal.

## Integrative Wellness
Beyond architecture, we offer a personalized approach to health:
- **OKEARA**: Access to structured hydrogen water with powerful antioxidant properties.
- **Smart Oases**: The "Neyra" spaces harmonize sound, light, and scent, responding to the guest's emotional energy.

![Nature Immersion](/portfolio/elementis/3.discover.png)

## Global Footprint
From our flagship location in **Ubud, Bali** (opening Q1 2025) to future developments in **Chile**, **Thailand**, and **New Zealand**, Elementis is expanding the boundaries of holistic wellness.

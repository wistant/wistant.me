# Freaking Delicious Protein + Caffeine ⚡

**Live life to the fullest with SPYLT**: Shatter boredom and embrace your inner kid with every deliciously smooth chug. Splyt is a personality-driven landing page that acts as a digital megaphone for a rebellious performance beverage brand.

![Splyt Brand Experience](/portfolio/splyt/splyt.webp)

## The Story
Rev up your rebel spirit and feed the adventure of life with SPYLT, where you’re one chug away from epic nostalgia and fearless fun. We built a heavily stylized showcase for their *6 freaking delicious flavors*, from Max Chocolate Milk to Peanut Butter Chocolate.

![Splyt Nutrients Interface](/portfolio/splyt/splyt2.webp)

## It Still Does Body Good
Beneath the wild branding lies a serious nutritional powerhouse. The frontend beautifully presents the raw math of the product without feeling like a medical diagram:
- **Potassium**: Up to 245mg
- **Calcium**: Up to 500mg
- **Vitamins A & D**
- **Iron Support** to fuel your journey.

# Live Gutsy: Soda Perfected 🍒

**Fizzi** is a highly energetic landing page that introduces a perfect modern soda: *3-5g sugar, 9g fiber, and 5 delicious flavors*. Dive into better health and enjoy refreshment without the guilt.

![Fizzi First Flavor Showcase](/portfolio/fizzi/fizzi1.webp)

## The Interactive Physics Playground
Fizzi features a heavily animated environment utilizing **GSAP**. As you scroll, vibrant 3D soda cans float, rotate, and interact with the typography, creating a tactile digital experience that reflects the product's effervescence.

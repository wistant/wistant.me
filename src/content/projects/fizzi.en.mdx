## The Interactive Physics Playground

![Fizzi First Flavor Showcase](/portfolio/fizzi/fizzi1.webp)

Fizzi features a heavily animated physics-like environment utilizing **GSAP**. As the user scrolls, the vibrant 3D soda cans float, rotate, and interact with the typography. 

![Fizzi Black Cherry](/portfolio/fizzi/fizzi2.webp)

This smooth, highly optimized animation technique makes the cans look perfectly integrated into the HTML layout while allowing users to discover flavors like **Black Cherry** (12 cans - $35.99) with zero lag.

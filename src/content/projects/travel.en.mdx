# Travel: Explore the World 🌍

**Travel** is a digital compass for the modern explorer. We curate breathtaking destinations and provide the tools to make your next big adventure a reality.

![Travel Experience](/portfolio/travel/travel.landing.webp)

## The Story
The world is too big to stay in one place. Our platform is designed to inspire curiosity and provide the clarity needed to navigate the globe’s most beautiful corners. We don't just show destinations; we tell stories of discovery.

## Features
- **Curated Guides**: Hand-picked locations for every type of traveler.
- **Immersive Visuals**: High-definition galleries that bring the world to your screen.
- **Seamless Planning**: Effortless tools to organize your journey from flight to stay.

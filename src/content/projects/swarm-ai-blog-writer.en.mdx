# Swarm AI Orcherstration

This project demonstrates the power of multi-agent systems in content creation. By piping specialized agents, we achieve a depth and structure that single-prompt systems often struggle with.

## The Vision
The goal was to create a research-assistant that doesn't just write, but plans and verifies. Pydantic ensures that the "Planner" doesn't just hallucinate a structure, but follows a strict JSON schema that the "Writer" can reliably consume.
